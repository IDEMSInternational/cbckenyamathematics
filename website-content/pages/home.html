Grade 10 CBC Mathematics Project

Welcome to the Grade 10 CBC Mathematics Project. It aims to facilitate collaboration between teachers, educators, and academics to build on existing resources for the teaching and learning of Grade 10 Mathematics in Kenya.

About the Project

The team behind the project has created a set of teaching and learning resources that span the entire Grade 10 CBC Mathematics curriculum.

These resources are organised into three complementary strands:

Interactive textbook

An online interactive textbook that spans the entire curriculum, with carefully designed questions that provide instantaneous targeted feedback to students. The textbook can also be available in offline and printable formats.

See resources

Training courses

A set of certified training courses that support teachers to navigate Curriculum Based Mathematics education, and guides on how to review, contribute to, and strengthen the resources.

See resources

Lesson plan courses

Full lesson plans and step-by-step guides for each topic in the curriculum, and corresponding certified courses to help improve the resources collaboratively and help the team understand the different needs in the various contexts in Kenya.

See resources
