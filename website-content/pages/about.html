About the Project

The CBC Kenya Mathematics project is a comprehensive initiative to support Grade 10 mathematics teachers with high-quality, accessible educational resources aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyan mathematics teachers with the tools, resources, and knowledge they need to deliver exceptional mathematics education that develops critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and mathematical competencies in students.

The CBC Approach

The Competency-Based Curriculum represents a shift from content-focused to competency-focused education. Our resources are designed to help teachers:

  • Develop students' critical thinking and problem-solving abilities
  • Create engaging, learner-centered classroom experiences
  • Assess student competencies effectively
  • Connect mathematical concepts to real-world applications
  • Foster collaborative learning environments
